# Privacy Policy for Sentimental Moments

**Last Updated: July 2026**

Your privacy is critically important to us.

## 1. Information We Collect
Sentimental Moments ("the App") collects data you provide directly: text logs, photos, voice recordings, and metadata (like timestamps and categories).

## 2. How We Store Your Data
By default, all your data is stored **locally on your mobile device**. We do not have access to your memories, photos, or voice notes.

If you choose to use a Backup & Sync feature in the future, your data will be transmitted securely and stored in your linked personal cloud account (such as Google Drive or iCloud). We do not store your memories on our own servers.

## 3. Device Permissions
To provide its core features, the App requests the following permissions on your device:
*   **Camera and Photo Library**: To allow you to attach photos to your memories.
*   **Microphone**: To allow you to record and attach voice notes to your memories.
These features process data locally on your device.

## 4. Analytics & Diagnostics
We may collect anonymous usage data and crash reports to improve the App. This data does not contain any of your personal memories, photos, voice recordings, or identifiable information.

## 5. Third-Party Services
We do not share your memories with third parties. Authentication and backup features may utilize third-party APIs subject to their respective privacy policies.
